Replate needs your help today!

We use data science and our proven technology to get food from those who find themselves with too much to people who find themselves with too little. With the closure of offices nation wide, our supply of surplus food has all but dried up while food insecurity is on the rise. You can help us get meals to those most affected by the current public health crisis.

What does my donation provide?

Your donations of food allow us to help you put the food you have spent so much money and effort preparing to good use when you can not consume all of it in your business operations. It reduces the enviromental impact of disposing of the food through incineration or in a landfill, and reduces the contries terrible statistics on food wastage.

Monetary donations help us run our office and the servers and other technology that make it possible for us to continue to operate. They pay for the purchase and maintenance of the vehicles our volunteers use to pick-up your food. They also pay for the continual upgrading of our software to make it even easier to continue our service.
